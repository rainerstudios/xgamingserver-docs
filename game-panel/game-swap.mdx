## **Overview**

This guide helps you navigate XGamingServer's supported Minecraft server types, including **Vanilla**, **Modded**, and **Optimized** variants. Use the filters below to find your ideal setup.

## **Categories**

## **Choosing Your Server Type**

1. **Performance Needs**
   - Small servers: Vanilla/Spigot
   - 50\+ players: Paper/Purpur
   - 100\+ players: Pufferfish/Folia
2. **Mod Requirements**
   - Client-side: Fabric/Quilt
   - Server-side: Forge
   - Hybrid: Arclight
3. **Network Setup**
   - Single server: Paper
   - Multi-server: Velocity \+ BungeeCord
